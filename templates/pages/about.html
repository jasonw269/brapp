Who We Are

Founded in the heart of the community, the Beeston Rylands Archers has been bringing people together through the sport of archery for decades. Our club prides itself on being welcoming, inclusive and passionate about the sport.

We are affiliated with Archery GB and follow their rules and safety guidelines. All our coaches hold recognised qualifications and our facilities are regularly inspected.
